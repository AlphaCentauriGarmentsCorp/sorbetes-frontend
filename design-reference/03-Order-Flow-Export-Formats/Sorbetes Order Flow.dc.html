State · sample_approval

Your sample is ready

Approve it to move to the 60% downpayment, or request changes. The seller classifies each change:

Minor
Fixed in-house at no extra cost, then straight to downpayment.
Major
Another ₱1,000 (separate, not credited) + a remade sample.

Minor fix — on the house

The warehouse adjusts the sample in-house at no extra fee. Once it's tweaked, you proceed straight to the 60% downpayment.

Major revision — remake

A bigger change means a fresh sample. A new ₱1,000 sample fee applies (separate, not credited to your total), then we produce a revised sample for review.
